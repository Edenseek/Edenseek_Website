About Edenseek

Stories Born Different.

Edenseek Publishing is an independent publisher of cinematic comics and children's books. We build original worlds — and we put the artwork first.

What we make

Our comic line tells mature, story-driven tales with film-grade art — from the sci-fi thriller I Ride for Them to the noir-soaked Society of Killers. Our children's line brings warmth and wonder to young readers through Egypt the Cat, a picture-book series about a magical little cat.

How we work

Every Edenseek title is a collaboration across borders — writers, artists, colorists, and editors working together from Detroit to Milan to New York. We credit every creator by role, because the people who make the work are as important as the work itself.

Born different

Our name and our spirit come from the same idea: the best stories don't fit a mold. They're born different — and so are we.
