Egypt the Cat

A magical little cat, a curious boy, and adventures that leap off the page.

Egypt the Cat is a bright, whimsical picture-book series for young readers, following a spirited orange cat and his best friend as they tumble through magic shows, dance floors, road trips, time itself, and the far-off future. Created by young storyteller Ness Uskert and brought to life by illustrator Michael Bryan Quiambao, Egypt the Cat celebrates wonder, friendship, and being born different.

The collection

The books

Five adventures: Magic! Magic! Magic!, Dance Maniac, Leaves Los Angeles, In Time, and In the Future.

Creative team
