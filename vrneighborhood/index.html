VR Neighborhood

A Legacy Social VR Game

VR Neighborhood was a legacy social VR game available on the Oculus Store from July 2023 to December 2025.

Originally launched as an independent virtual reality platform, it explored persistent social worlds, shared spaces, and immersive community experiences.

Today, the ideas behind VR Neighborhood continue under the Edenseek umbrella as part of the emerging Edenseek Spatial initiative.

A Legacy That Continues

VR Neighborhood helped shape the early thinking behind Edenseek's broader vision: stories, worlds, characters, and communities that can extend across books, comics, games, artificial intelligence, and immersive computing.

Where we're headed

Edenseek Spatial is being designed as a next-generation platform for:

  • Immersive storytelling
  • Creator-owned virtual worlds
  • AI-assisted characters
  • Shared persistent environments
  • Publishing across books, comics, games, and VR

The vision has expanded, but the original mission remains the same:

Help people build meaningful worlds together.
