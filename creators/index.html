The talent

The people behind the worlds.

Edenseek brings together creators spanning four continents and five decades of experience — from Eisner Award winners to first-time published talent. Our writers, artists, colorists, letterers, and editors come from every walk of life, bringing a wealth of experience, perspective, and creativity to every story we publish.
